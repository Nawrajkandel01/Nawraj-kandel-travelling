Authentic Travel at Fair Prices

Our primary focus is on organizing authentic travel experiences at fair prices. We determine the prices of our trips based on actual expenses, considering factors such as hotel category, entry tickets, transportation, and meals. By booking at honest prices, our guests enjoy the advantage of touring with a local Kathmandu-based tour company.

Ideal Trip Itineraries

Embark on a journey of Exclusive Private Trips, Small Group Tours, and Family and Luxury Holidays that unveil the wonders of the Himalayas. Immerse yourself in captivating destinations, explore hidden gems, and partake in curated activities that showcase the essence of each attraction. From cultural delights to trekking adventures, our itineraries ensure you make the most of every moment.

Travel with a skillful Leader

Our trip Leaders are not only professional and friendly guides but highly skillful in their roles! Exclusive private trips, Small Group Tours, and Family Holidays are led by different guides based on their experiences, expertise & recent guest recommendations. Our dedicated leaders bring local knowledge and passion to every tour, ensuring an authentic and unforgettable journey.

Customized Group Size

Depending on the season, nature, and style of the trip, our Small Group Tours typically accommodate 10 to 16 guests in a group. Among Small Group Tours; Trekking and Active Tours have a maximum limit of 10 guests and Sightseeing and Exploratory Tours are limited to a maximum of 16 guests per group. Any group size is welcome for Exclusive trips and family holidays.
